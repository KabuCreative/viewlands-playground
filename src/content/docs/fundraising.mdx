Transforming the Viewlands playground is an ambitious multi-year project. As the school has a very limited internal budget, this project relies almost entirely on external grants, corporate partnerships, and community fundraising.

## Our Funding Roadmap

Based on the indicative costs discussed by our working group, we have set a long-term target of **£200,000 – £250,000** for a full redevelopment. To ensure this is realistic and deliverable, we are working through a phased approach.

<Steps>

1.  **Phase 1: "Quick Wins" & Safety** <Badge text="Current Focus" variant="tip" />
    Funded primarily through the Parent Council and local community grants. This phase addresses immediate safety repairs, the P1–P3 seating, and early landscaping.

2.  **Phase 2: Nature & Social Hubs**
    Focused on sensory play, slope development, and initial shelter structures. This phase will require mid-tier grant applications and corporate material support.

3.  **Phase 3: The Full Vision**
    Our long-term goal for installing play equipment and major infrastructure. This phase relies on successful applications to Scotland's largest funding bodies.

</Steps>

## Strategic Funding Pillars

### 1. Professional Design & "Seed Funding"

As discussed in our February meeting, professional input is vital to avoid costly mistakes and strengthen our case for investment. We are looking to secure **National Lottery seed funding** to establish a clear vision for play, which will act as a springboard for larger grant applications.

### 2. Corporate Match-funding

Many local employers in Perth—including **SSE** and major financial institutions—offer match-funding schemes. If a parent or carer raises money for the playground, their employer may match that amount pound-for-pound. This is one of our most effective ways to double our impact.

### 3. Parent Council & Community Support

The **Viewlands Parent Council** is the core supporter of our "Quick Wins." By funding early, visible improvements, we can demonstrate to major grant bodies that our community is fully committed and that the project is already delivering results.

---

## Transparency & Governance

Every pound raised is dedicated to the playground redevelopment.

- **Financial Oversight:** All funds are managed through the **Viewlands Parent Council**, which provides full financial oversight and transparency.
- **Track Record:** We are building on recent successes, such as the works supported by the Westbank Project, to prove that we can deliver high-quality improvements efficiently.
