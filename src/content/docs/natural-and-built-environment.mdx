## Site-Specific Challenges

Our landscaping considerations are shaped by the specific conditions of the Viewlands grounds.

### 1. Weather & Shelter

The "top of the hill" location leaves the playground exposed to the elements. We are exploring the introduction of shelter and weather protection to help pupils and staff escape the wind and rain.

### 2. Mud & Surface Management

Deteriorating grass and mud build-up are major issues during wet weather.

- **Woodchip:** We are investigating the suitability of woodchip for the most affected muddy areas.
- **The Slope:** We are exploring how to make better use of the grass slope, potentially for low-risk climbing or sliding features, provided ground conditions can be managed.

### 3. Security & Vandalism

Given that the Viewlands nursery has suffered from vandalism in the past, all new-build elements must be designed for durability. We are focused on using vandal-resistant materials and ensuring layouts maintain clear lines of sight for supervision.

### 4. Impact on Neighbours

Any new landscaping or fencing will be carefully considered to ensure it does not negatively impact the privacy, light, or views of our school neighbours.
