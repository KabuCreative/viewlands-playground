## Project Partners

**Contribution:** Joinery & Site Maintenance

Westbank is a Perth-based provider of educational and employability-related activities. They support clients with significant barriers to employment by facilitating introductory courses in joinery, horticulture, and land-based industries.

As part of their commitment to community support, Westbank has been a vital partner in our "Quick Wins" phase by:

- **Joinery:** Building and installing our heavy-duty, upcycled picnic benches and planters.
- **Site Maintenance:** Carrying out the safe removal of aged and broken playground equipment.

**Contribution:** Supply of building materials

Through the **Wickes Community Programme**, the Perth store has provided vital material support for our early-stage improvements. This initiative helps local community groups and schools transform their spaces by donating essential products and timber.

Wickes has been a key supporter of our "Quick Wins" phase by:

- **Timber Donations:** Supplying the high-quality, treated timber required for our new planters and seating.

## Community & Local Support

In addition to our core partners, we are supported by the energy and dedication of the Viewlands community.

### Viewlands Parent Council

The Parent Council provides the essential governance and early-stage funding that allows this project to move forward. Their commitment to "Quick Wins" has provided the seed money required to begin site improvements and demonstrate momentum to national funders.
