## Our School Grounds Today

Located at one of the highest points in Perth, Viewlands Primary enjoys a prominent position, but our playground currently faces significant challenges.

The existing space is largely dominated by asphalt, leaving the grounds feeling **sparse and exposed** to the wind and rain. While our school community is vibrant, the physical environment often falls short of modern play standards. Key issues we are addressing include:

- **Weather Exposure:** A lack of shelter at the "top of the hill" limits outdoor play during wet and windy periods.
- **The "Mud Factor":** Poor drainage and deteriorating grass surfaces make large areas of the site unusable for much of the school year.
- **Play Balance:** A lack of defined "zones" leads to high-energy games unintentionally dominating the space, leaving fewer options for quiet or creative play.

---

## The Vision: A Learning Landscape

We are working to transform these grounds into a multi-functional **Learning Landscape**. This is an ambitious, multi-year project with an estimated total cost of **£200,000 – £250,000**.

Our approach is built on two complementary workstreams: improving the physical infrastructure through landscaping and seating, and installing modern play equipment that encourages **managed risk** and physical development.
